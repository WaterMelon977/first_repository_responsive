About myself

Hello, I'm a 2018 graduate who embarked on the journey to crack the civil services exam. While I faced setbacks and didn't make it to the interview stage, I've embraced a new path and am currently honing my skills to become a web developer. With the support of friends, my transition into this field is progressing smoothly. Outside the world of coding, I find joy in chilling with friends and immersing myself in the timeless tunes of rock music, with Pink Floyd being a personal favorite. The call of the outdoors beckons me, and I often find myself on vacations, exploring picturesque destinations, with Kerala holding a special place in my heart.

Eager to earn my freedom, I'm investing my time and effort into acquiring the skills necessary for a successful career. Life threw challenges my way, especially in the pursuit of becoming a civil servant, but I've embraced the lessons learned and am now planning to win at life by accepting my losses and focusing on the journey ahead.
